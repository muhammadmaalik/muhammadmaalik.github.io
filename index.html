TVC (Thrust Vector Control)

Thrust Vector Control is simply using a mechanism that can control your rocket's flight trajectory. It is upto you to make it complex or simple. Using just a system of servos and a micro-controller can help you steer your rocket for the time it is in the air. To make it more advance you can add another Servo on the top of the rocket to make a lever for the parachute-which can save it from big heights.
